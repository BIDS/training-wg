Cathryn Carson

Cathryn Carson is a historian and ethnographer of contemporary science and technology. Before getting her PhD in history, she was trained in computational condensed matter physics. As Associate Dean of Social Sciences, she built D-Lab, which opened in 2013 and serves social scientists (and others) across campus doing data-intensive research.

Anthony Suen

Anthony is a project program analyst for the UC Berkeley Data Science Initiative and a BIDS fellow. Anthony co-founded the BIDS Collaborative, which helps students collaborate with non-profits, government, and research institutes to solve challenging real-world issues.

Carl Boettiger

Carl is an ecologist working on problems in ecological forecasting and decision theory. His research blends theoretical investigation with the creation of software tools to use and test these concepts in ecological questions.

Some of our current projects

Data Science Education Program

The UC Berkeley Data Science education program seeks to make the ability to understand and formulate reasoning based on data accessible to all Berkeley students. Even before the first pilot offerings, a team of students were working to understand how such a program might be designed to best provide what students view as essential to their college experience.

Hacker Within

This is a collaborative peer-driven weekly meeting for sharing skills and best practices for scientific computation. BIDS Collaborative

BIDS Collaborative hopes to develop innovative data science solutions to challenges in academia, government, non-profits, and industry while also creating a pipeline of students for successful future careers in data science.

Diversity in Data Science

Encouraging diversity in Data Science has been a main focus; often times, students of various majors who have had little to no experience programming will find Data Science intimidating, and will therefore choose not to pursue it. Through the new Data Science Scholars program, Data Science has been able to become a much more inclusive space.
