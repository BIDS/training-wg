Cathryn Carson

Cathryn Carson is a historian and ethnographer of contemporary science and technology. Before getting her PhD in history, she was trained in computational condensed matter physics. As Associate Dean of Social Sciences, she built D-Lab, which opened in 2013 and serves social scientists (and others) across campus doing data-intensive research.

Anthony Suen

Anthony is a project program analyst for the UC Berkeley Data Science Initiative and a BIDS fellow. Anthony co-founded the BIDS Collaborative, which helps students collaborate with non-profits, government, and research institutes to solve challenging real-world issues.

Carl Boettiger

Carl is an ecologist working on problems in ecological forecasting and decision theory. His research blends theoretical investigation with the creation of software tools to use and test these concepts in ecological questions.
