Welcome to the BIDS Education Training Working Group!

The BIDS ETWG meets bi-monthly to discuss events and issues occuring within the Data Science at Berkeley community. Through our various projects, we encourage collaboration between undergraduate students, graduate students, and professors. Data Science has been a growing field at Berkeley the last couple of years, and there are many groups here at Berkeley who have made that possible. In addition to looking to expand our student team, the faculty at BIDS is always excited to speak to students who drop by.
